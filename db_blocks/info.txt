The "db_blocks" folder is required !!!. It will contain all generated block transactions of the form:

- db_blocks
--> block-NUMBER
---> (Example) Exodus: ab18252b7c3c48968b17169b6be14b25cd613dc2ce2b68250054a2c2bfea4532 | 
               Output: 4bf7d9921640d5f581d87f9e643726a4b5e3b8a1abb7b8af6700d6e2742c1e78
     ...
